# Worksheet 2 — My Rules (Layer 2: Protection & Allocation)

These are the non-negotiables generic AI always misses. You already have
them in your head — this is the first time you write them down where an AI
will check them before every answer. 3–5 rules is plenty to start.

```
TARGETS
  Emergency fund target:      ___ months of expenses
  Save / Spend / Invest:      ___% / ___% / ___%

PRIORITIES (in order — what gets money first?)
  1. _____________________________________________
  2. _____________________________________________
  3. _____________________________________________

NON-NEGOTIABLES (steal from below or write your own)
  □ Highest-interest debt gets every surplus peso first
  □ Never invest before the emergency fund hits its target
  □ Never carry a credit card balance past ___ days
  □ Any purchase over $_____: wait 48 hours
  □ Never invest in anything I can't explain in two sentences
  □ No lifestyle inflation on a good month
  □ Life insurance non-negotiable while I have dependents
  □ My own: _______________________________________
  □ My own: _______________________________________
```

Prompt if stuck: *what money rule do you already enforce on yourself that
no app or advisor knows about?*

When done, this becomes your `rules.md`.
