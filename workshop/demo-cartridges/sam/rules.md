# Rules — Sam

> Demo persona. All rules are illustrative.

## Targets

- Emergency fund: 6 months of expenses (≈$240,000) — freelance income, so
  double the usual cushion
- Split target: 15% save / 55% spend / 30% invest

## Priorities (in order, non-negotiable)

1. Emergency fund stays at 6 months — never below
2. Cash above the emergency fund is a problem, not a comfort: it must be
   put to work (inflation eats idle pesos)
3. Income protection gap: as a freelancer, evaluate disability insurance
   before increasing lifestyle spend

## Non-negotiables

- Never invest in anything I can't explain to a friend in two sentences
- Any purchase over $5,000: wait 48 hours before buying
- No lifestyle inflation on a good month — surplus follows the split, not
  the mood
