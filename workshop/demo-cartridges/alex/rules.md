# Rules — Alex

> Demo persona. All rules are illustrative.

## Targets

- Emergency fund: 3 months of expenses (≈$66,000) before any investing
- Split target once debt-free: 20% save / 60% spend / 20% invest

## Priorities (in order, non-negotiable)

1. Highest-interest debt first — the 45% credit card before anything else
2. Emergency fund to 3 months
3. Only then: investing

## Non-negotiables

- Never carry a credit card balance past 60 days
- Never skip the car loan payment (needed for work)
- No new debt while the credit card has a balance
- Any purchase over $3,000: wait 48 hours before buying
