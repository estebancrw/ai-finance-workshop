# After the Workshop — Keeping Your Money KB Alive

You left with a Level-1 Snapshot KB. Its value now depends on one habit.

## The refresh ritual (15 min, monthly) — Level 2

1. Open your `My Money KB` project.
2. Update `snapshot.md`: new balances, last month's spend, bump `as_of`.
3. Add **one** rule you learned about yourself this month (or none — rules
   change rarely, that's the point).
4. Ask the trend questions — the ones no generic chatbot can ever answer:
   - Am I better off than last month?
   - Is my "misc" shrinking?
   - Am I closer to my emergency fund target?
5. Keep your old snapshots. They are your history.

**If you only do one thing: keep the calendar event you created at the
workshop.** The refresh IS the system.

## Standing questions your KB can answer today

- Where does my next peso go?
- Am I protected? (emergency fund, insurance gaps)
- Where can I cut? What's actually in my "misc"?
- Am I saving/investing enough against my own targets?
- I'm about to buy X — does it break any of my rules?

## The guardrail — worth remembering

AI **drafts**, you **execute**. Anything irreversible — money moved,
message sent, commitment made — a human approves first. (Air Canada's
chatbot once invented a refund policy; a tribunal made them honor it.
Don't let your AI make promises on your behalf.)
